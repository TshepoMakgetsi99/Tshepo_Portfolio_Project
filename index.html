About me

I am energetic about my work. Since I love what I do, I have a consistent wellspring of inspiration that drives me to put forth a valiant effort. I am an ADA
Graduate

My Journey at The African Developers Academy was fruitful. I joined ADA July 2019 as a student with no coding experience. The level of excitement was sky high, what's more, the warm,well disposed and inviting team helped to increase my confidence and happiness in the program. As the first week went by I felt that I really deserved to be in this wonderful team. I have had quite the journey learning how to become a front-end developer, putting me under great strain at times, but overall, it has inspired me to grow.
